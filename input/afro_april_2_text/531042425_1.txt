NINE BOYS SENT
TO CHELTENHAM
The largest group convicted in
the Juvenile Court and committed
to an institution were the nine
boys who were sent to Cheltenham
Reform School, this week.
The boys were arraigned before
Judge Thomas Waxter. They were
cited on misdemeanors as second
offenders. The length of con-
finement ranged from one to three
years.

were:
