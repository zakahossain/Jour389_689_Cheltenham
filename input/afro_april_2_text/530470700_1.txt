Took Boys to Omaha
Miss Anita Williams, social
worker with the local Catholic
Welfare Organizations, returned
Sunday from Omaha, Neh., where
she went to take three hoys to the
Father Funnagin's Home for
Boys.
The boys ranging in ages from
eight to 12 were formerly inmates
of the St. Elizabeth Home and
were sent to the Nebraska insti-
tution to avoid having them go to
Cheltenham.
Miss Williams pointed out the
need for an institution nearer here
for the proper care of this class
of boys.
