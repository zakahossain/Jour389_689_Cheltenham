20 Cent Loot Sends
Boy To Cheltenham
With three previous records against
him, Ernest Thompson, 15, 610 Ma-
deria street, was com.mitted to Chel-
tenham School of Reformation when
arraigned before Judge Williams, in
Juvenile Court, Thursday.
Thompson, according to testimony.
is a member of a gang of young des-
peradoes who have committed nu-
merous major crimes in the Eastern
District. Late Sunday evening, in
company with three other older boys
he held up George Capper. 126 Pratt
street, at the point of pistols and
knives while he was standing at the
corner of Pratt and Albermarle
street. Then the boys took twenty
cents and a car check from the man
which was all he had on his person
at the time. Police attracted to the
scene, chased-the youth and cap-
tured Thompson. He will remain in
the reformatory until he is 21 years
of age.
