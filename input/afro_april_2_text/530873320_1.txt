Francis M. Wood, director of the
Baltimore public schools, returned to
the city Wednesday from a five
-weeks' trip abroad with the Hamp-
ton tourists.
Reached at his office Dr. Wood re-
ported a highly interesting journey
through England, France, Germany.
Holland and Belgium. He will get
back on the job Monday of next
week, he says. It is expected that
appointments and changes in the
public schools, which have been
awaiting the return of Dr. Wood will
be the first thing to be given at-
tention. Dr. Wood will give some of
the impressions of his trip in next
week's AFRO.
