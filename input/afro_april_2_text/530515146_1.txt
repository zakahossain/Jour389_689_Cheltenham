Jamaicans Arrive
Seven Young Women Here To
Seck Their Fortunes.
Seven young women from
Jamaica were passengers on the
Norwegian steamer Machioneal.
which arrived in port last Friday
with a cargo of fruit. They were
Misses Esther McEwan, Gertrude
Carmen Tafe, Florence Evelinia
Heightington,
Una Gertrude
Scott, Minna Sutherland William-
son. Edna May Atkinson and
Doris Isabell Atkinson.
Miss. Taffe will enter a higher
school in this country. Miss Doris
Sutherland went to Boston and
Miss Taffe to Montclair, N. J. The
others went to New York city.
Miss Doris Isabel Atkinson gave
her occupation as an accountant,
and the remainder, except Miss
Taffe, said they were dressmakers.
