According to the report of the
committee which visited the institu-
tion on March 1, all boys under the
age of 16 were found in class rooms
here and were being instructed in
regular-organized classes by appar-
ently competent teachers.
"The instructors seemed gentle-
manly and efficient and lacked all
semblance of the 'hard-boiled' type
generally found in such institutions,"
the report says.

Older boys were found at work in

ing ably taught.
