Cheltenham Gets $80,000 Gym
Here's a view of the new $80,000 gym-
nasium at the Cheltenham School for
Boys, which will be dedicated on Sun-
day afternoon, October 20. This build-
ing has a floor space 94 by 53 feet and
is equipped with facilities for the popu-
lar indoor games. It has showers, lava-
tories and dressing rooms on the base-
ment floor, which is not complete, pend-
ing the granting of additional funds for
a swimming pool.
Here's an interior view of the new gym at the Chelten-
ham School for Boys in Prince Georges County, Md.
Note the balcony which, besides providing for a few
spectators, also has a stand for the support of a small
moving-picture camera. The gym floor is 94 by 53 feet
and is suitable for basketball, boxing, volley ball, bad-
minton and trapeze exercises. The basement will have
showers, lavatories, dressing rooms, and a swimming
pool.
