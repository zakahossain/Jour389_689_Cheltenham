Say Youth Looted
Paper Cash Boxes

Police are keeping a watchout for
a gang of youths who are said to
have tampered with and robbed 90
honor system paper boxes, last week.
The youth who are known to police
are believed to be working under the
direction of older boys.

Authorities of the Cheltenham Re-
formatory notified police to appro-
hend Bernard Robinson, alias
“peaches” and hold him as an escapee
from that institution. The youth is
described as being 15 years of age, 5
feet one inch tall, 115 pounds in
weight, gingercake complexion, brown
eyes, and with a scar on his upperlip.
At the time of his escape the boy was
wearing the customary blue uniform
pants with a brown jumper, black
shoes and no hat.
