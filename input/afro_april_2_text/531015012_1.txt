Three Boys Held
Three boys who were arraigned
in the Juvenile Court on charges
made by policemen were ordered
held for the Cheltenham Reform
School by Magistrate Thomas E.
Waxter, this week.
The boys' names were given as
Bernard Johnson, 13, 513 Orchard
Street; George Newson, Spring St.,
Robert Smith, 14. 1032 Gillis Court;
Alfred Haskins, 418 N. Carey St.
