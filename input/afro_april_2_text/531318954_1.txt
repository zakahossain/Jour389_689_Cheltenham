New Cheltenham
Head Takes Over
BALTIMORE
Vance E. Thomas, white, for-
mer parole officer at the Fed-
eral Penitentiary at Atlanta, Ga.,
took over his new duties as su-
perintendent at the Cheltenham
School for Boys on Saturday, it
was announced this week.
Mr. Thomas was selected from
a list of eligible persons who
passed a State exam early this
year. He succeeds B. B. Sunder-
land, also white, who has re-
turned to his former position as
parole officer of the school.
