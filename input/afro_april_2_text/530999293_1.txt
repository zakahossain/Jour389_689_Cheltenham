AFRO Editor Named
on Md. N.R.A. Body
President Roosevelt Names
Carl J. Murphy on State
Committee.
WASHINGTON. Carl J. Murphy,
editor of THE AFRO-AMERICAN,
was among those named by Presi-
dent Roosevelt to serve on the Mary-
land State N.R.A. Committee to co-
ordinate the recovery program in
the State and work with the Federal
and State authorities in furthering
the program.
Others named on the committee
were, W. Mason Sheehan, of Easton;
William E. Robinson, of Belair;
Archbishop Michael Curley, Donald
Symington, John J. Nelligan, Charles
H. Cohn, Albert D. Hutzler and
Henry F. Broening, all white.
