Five Youths
Convicted Here
Convicted on charges of assault
larceny or burglary, five boys were
ordered sent to Cheltenham Reform-
atory after a hearing in the Juve-
nile Court, this week.
Eugene James, 16, 5339 Denmore
Avenue, was committed for four
years when convicted of assaulting
two white girls near Pimlico. The
others committed were: John Ben-
nett, 13,893 Park Avenue, Edward
Brown, 17, 423 Worsley Street
Frank Hopkins, 15, 910 Myrtle Ave-
nue, and Edward Barclay, 15, 743
George Street.
David Slaughter, 12, 2315 Hun-
ter Street, was cited by Northern
District Police on charges of truancy
preferred by Mrs. Rosabel E. Hall,
chief attendance officer for Public
Schools.

Find Body of Babe
The crew of the Police boat, Rob-
ert D. Carter, found the body of a
day old male baby floating in the
harbor at Eastern Avenue and East
Falls Avenue, Thursday. The body
was sighted by members of the crew
of the dredge ship National.
