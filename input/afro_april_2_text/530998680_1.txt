Two Boys Sent to
Reform School
Convicted of criminal offenses,
two boys were ordered committed to
the Cheltenham Reform School
from juvenile court, this week.
The boys were sentenced from one
to three years by Magistrate Thom-
as Waxter. They gave their names
as Elmore Bailey, 13, 1051 W. Fay-
ette Street, and James Reid, 13,
1007 W. Fayette Street.
