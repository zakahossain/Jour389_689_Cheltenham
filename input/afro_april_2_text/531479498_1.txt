New Divorce Grounds
Under terms of a case decided
in the Circuit Court here recent-.
ly, when either party refuses to
agree to the hearing of children,
that constitutes marital desertion.
This was established in the case
of Ann C. Malley vs. Bernard Mal-
ley, in which the wife testified
that her husband insisted on use
of contraceptives, although they
had agreed before marriage to
have three children.
