Jim Crow Foe
Gets 4 Years
| BALTIMORE.
--
Charles
Butcher, 26, white, ousted em-
ployee of the Cheltenham School
for Boys, on Monday received a
four-year sentence in the Federal
Court for refusing to obey an or-
der to report to a conscientious
objectors' camp.
A Harvard graduate, Butcher
began working at Cheltenham in
November. He was released from
the institution in January on
three charges, one of them being
his insistence on eating in a din-
ing hall reserved for colored em-
ployees.
Butcher stated that he had been
offered several jobs since his dis-
missal from Cheltenham, but de-
clined them because he wanted to
do what he could in straighten-
ing out the racial situation at the
institution.
He said that the case is a "test"
for clarification of Selective Serv-
ice regulations concerning con-
scientious objectors and would be
carried to the United States Su-
preme Court, if necessary.
Judge Coleman refused to grant
Butcher bail, pending a ruling on
his appeal.
