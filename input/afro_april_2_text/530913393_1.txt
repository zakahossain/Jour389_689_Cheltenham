fered to them by the larger churches

Lindbergh Baby
The ministers denounced the tactics
employed by officials in the search for
the Lindbergh baby. What sort of
religion can we preach to the people
if we are going to condone the use of
unlawful methods in righting a wrong
such as the kidnaping of this child,
declared the Rev. Charles Stewart.
The minister further declared that
the employment of racketeers in the
search for the child, showed a break-
down in the moral fibre of the forces
of law and order in this country.
