BALTIMORE-A total of 174
persons, including twenty em-
ployees of the Cheltenham School
for Boys, were granted State
scholarships to study in colleges
and universities outside the State
by the scholarship committee of
the Morgan State College Board
of Trustees last week.
The scholarships are made pos-
sible by a $25,000 grant from the
State to enable students desiring
higher education in fields not
available at Morgan or Princess
Anne to secure it in other ac-
credited schools at State ex-
pense.
Recommendations for the
awards totaling $14,172 were
made after the committee, com-
posed of Dr. Ivan E. McDougle,
white, Josiah Henry, Jr., and Carl
Murphy, held interviews with the
new applicants.
