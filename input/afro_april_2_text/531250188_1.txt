AFRO Staff Artist
Is Father of Son
BALTIMORE
Mr. and Mrs. Francis L. Yan-
cey of 719 N. Carrollton Avenue
became the parents of an 8-pound
son, early Sunday morning.
The baby, who is to be chris-
tened Francis Edward Yancey,
was born in Woburn, Mass..
where Mrs. Yancey had been vis-
iting her aunt, Mrs. Susan Saun-
ders.
Mrs. Yancey, a member of the
Smart Set Social Club, is expect-
ed to return to Baltimore in May.
The new father is staff artist
for the AFRO.
Writ Frees Boy, 17
from Cheltenham
BALTIMORE
On the contention that he was
not given a proper trial when
sentenced to Cheltenham School
for Boys on July 7, 1937, Ed-
ward E. Wesley, 17, of 1710 N.
Mount Street was released from
the institution at a writ of
habeas corpus hearing in the
Court of Common Pleas, Thurs-
day.
