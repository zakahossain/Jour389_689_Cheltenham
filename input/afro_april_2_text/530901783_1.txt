BOYS SET FIRE TO
CHELTENHAM
WASHINGTON -Three boys, in
mates of the Maryland House of Re-
formation at Cheltenham, were ar-
rested Monday and charged with
having started the $5,000 fire which
swept the broom factory at the in-
stitution on March 10.
Police say the trio have confessed
and implicated a fourth lad whose
name is withheld pending his arrest.
Those held are: Granville Haskill,
Granville
15; George Johnson, 17, and Thomas
Deshields, 16.
The boys are said to have admitted
throwing blazing rags through a win-
dow into heaps of broom straw. The
plant was badly damaged by the fire
and a stock of finished brooms and
raw material were destroyed.
