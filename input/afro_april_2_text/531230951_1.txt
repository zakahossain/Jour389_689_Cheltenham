the uncrowned king of the fiddle,
is scheduled to make connections
with a big-name combo.
Richmond Ford is laying some
tall ivory at the Pioneer and the
joint is rockin' in rhythm.
From downtown, the Toby
Harris-Bob Chestnut duo at the
Venice are tops, Toby doing some
mighty fine vocalizing.
'Tis rumored that these lads
may don white ties and tails for
a turn at the exclusive Brown
Derby.
At that rendezvous 'round the
Fens, Pops Joyner and his boys
are still prime favorites.
'Tis reported that Jimmy (Deb-
onair) Love is now to be found
nitely at the Neck Grill in Sulli-
van Square and they do say his
inimitable style of putting over
songs has caught on like wild-
