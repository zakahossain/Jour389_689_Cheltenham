HATS OFF DEPARTMENT
AN EDITORIAL
Hats off to Brigadier General P. L. Sadler, new
commander of U.S. troops in Liberia, who last week
put an end to all jim crow mess halls and quarters for
officers.
To the Board of Managers of Cheltenham, Mary-
land's State reform school for colored boys, which did
the same thing for its colored and white personnel.
To Billy Curran and C. Arthur Eby, who know
discrimination in selecting juries when they see it.
To Darryl Zanuck, Hollywood producer, who was
delighted to share a North African trench with the big-
gest Senegalese soldier he had ever seen.
