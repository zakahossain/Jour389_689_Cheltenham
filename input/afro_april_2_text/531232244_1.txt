Illegally Held Youths
Home for Christmas
BALTIMORE
-
Through legal
procedures by which it was shown
they were being held at Chelten-
ham illegally, two youths were
released in time to celebrate
Christmas with their parents, last
week.
The boys were: John Johnson,.
12. of the 400 block Colvin Street,
and Albert Robinson, 13. of 866
Vine Street. The legal papers
were filed by John's mother, Mrs...
Martha Johnson, through George
W. Evans, attorney, and by Al-..
bert's father, George Robinson,
through George L. Fornoff, white
attorney.
