Another Precedent Broken
Another deviation from what
has been the case in the past is
the fact that the 1941 horroil
includes a person, Policewoman
Whyte, who has previously been
on the honor roll. However, be-
cause of the fact that she was
the first woman in recent years
to be cited for meritorious service
with the police department, it was
felt that she should be included.
She is sharing honors with De-
tective Patrolman Butler, who
was similarly honored.
