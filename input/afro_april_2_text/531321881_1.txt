Appropriation $30,000 Short
In its request to the governor,
Morgan asked for an appropria-
tion of $229,780 from the State
for salaries and operating ex-
penses. It actually received $198,-
900, so that the governor's allow-
ance is $30,880 less than request-
ed.
In its request for new build-
ings, it asked for a $100,000 pow-
er plant and a $220,000 gymnas-
ium, and $40,000 for teachers'
cottages. The power plant was
granted.
Morgan also asked $8,000 for
library equipment and $8,000 for
scientific equipment. Nothing was
allowed for these two items. Mor-
gan asked the State for $50,000
for heat tunnels, water lines, and
campus lights. Nothing was al-
lowed for these items.

Maryland Scholarships
The $30,000 scholarship funds
providing State aid to stu-
dents who study outside Mary-
land was reduced by $5,000 which
was the amount previously used
for operating expenses. Morgan
State College trustees administer
the funds now without additional
cost.
