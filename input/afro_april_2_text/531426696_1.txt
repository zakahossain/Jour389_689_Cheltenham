Church News
The Rev. H. J. Bryant, pastor
of St. John AME Church, was
elected president of the AME
Ministers' Conference to succeed
the Rev. W. P. Mitchell of Trinity
Church.
Madison Street Presbyterian
Church dedicated a service flag
honoring twenty-seven members
who are in the armed forces.

Gets $10,000 for Injuries
Robert B. Johnson, 55, carpen-
ter, won a $10,000 City Court
verdict against Joseph H. Beers,
white, for permanent back injur-
ies suffered in an automobile ac-
cident.
F. Reid Isaac, white, president
of the Strawbridge Home for
Boys, was elected president of
the board of managers of Chel-
tenham School for Boys. He suc-
ceeded Joseph H. Blanford, white,
who resigned April 10 because of
the board's directive to abolish
racial segregation among colored
and white employees at the insti-
tution.
A drive to raise $75,000 to re-
model and furnish the new YW-
CA at 1912-16 Madison Avenue
will extend until June 30.
