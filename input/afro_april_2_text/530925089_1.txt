Convicted Boy Sent
to Reform School
Accused by police one youth was
convicted and three others were
cited for hearings when arraigned
in the Juvenile Court, this week.
Convicted by Magistrate Thomas
Waxter, as a second offender, Aug-
ustus Taliaferro, 12, 2403 Druid Hill
Avenue, was ordered committed to
the Cheltenham Reformatory, this
week.
Charged with snatching the poc-
ketbook of Miss Rose Guffin, 327 W.
Preston Street, Louis Brown, 9, 834
W. Lexington Street, was ordered
held for the action of the juvenile
court.
