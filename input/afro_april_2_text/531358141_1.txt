Father and Son
Burned Fighting Fire
BALTIMORE
Benjamin Johnson, 56, and his
son, Charles, 26, were burned
slightly, while fighting a fire in
their home at 1110 Pennsylvania
Avenue, late Tuesday.
Taken to Provident Hospital,
the father was treated for burns
of the right hand, arm and neck,
while the son received treatment
for burns of the hands.
