Five Boys Sent
to Reform School
Arraigned in the juvenile court
on charges of citizens and police-
men, five boys were ordered com-
mitted to the Cheltenham Reform
School after hearings before Judge
Thomas Waxter, this week.
The boys cited on charges of
violating state laws. were Warren
C. Davis. 12, 800 W. Lanvale St.;
Wilson Worrell, 13. 1217 Madison
Avenue: Alvin Ashby, 12, 901
Sterling Street: William Liggons,
1311 Mosher Street; and John R
Lambert, 1732 Ashland Avenue.
