Hurt When Brakes Locked
James Barrett, 26, 1211 W. Frank-
lin street, sustained injuries of the
face and scalp when the steering gear
of the automobile he was operating
locked, causing him to run into a
telegraph pole in front of 3811 Dal-
rymple avenue, Sunday.
