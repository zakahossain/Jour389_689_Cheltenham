The Rules
Here are the spanking game
rules:
(1) Write one paragraph only.
The shorter, the better. Not
more than 30 words. Use one
side of the paper only.
(2) Write with pencil or with
ink, but write plainly.
(3) For the best views on the
spanking of children the AFRO
will give $50 for first prize;
second prize, $10; third prize,
$5, and 35 $1 prizes.
(4) Send in as many answers
as you wish, but put each one
on a separate piece of paper.
(5) No answers received after
Saturday, July 2. Awards will
be announced in the AFRO of
July 9.
(6) Watch next week's AFRO
for announcement of a new
AFRO game. You'll be sur-
prised.
(7) Address your letters to the
