They Don't Like This Life Much
A few of the four hundred boys at the House of Reform-
ation at Cheltenham, Md. One of their group was shot
by a white guard last Saturday because he is alleged
to have said "something smart." Their is much unrest
among older inmates at the institution. Although officials
forbid any questioning of the boys, some of them openly
admitted to AFRO reporters that they "didn't like down
here much."
