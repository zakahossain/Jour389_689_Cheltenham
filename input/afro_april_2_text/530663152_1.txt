Money To Others
The home for the Blind at Overlea
received 50 pounds of candy. Twenty-
five dollars each went to St. Francis
Home For Girls, St. Elizabeth Home,
and the Mission Helpers on Biddle
street near McCulloh. The boys at
Cheltenham received 100 pounds of
candy.
The treat this year cost more than
$300, a sum slightly higher than in
past years. Contributions were re-
ceived from friends of the club, and
the secretary reports that a complete
list of their names will be published
in January.
