Many Destitute, Sent
to Institutions Here
Commitments to various institu-
tions were issued by police magis-
trates for twenty persons, including
one woman, who were arrested and
convicted of being without homes.
this week.
The lone woman to be cited on
charges of vagrancy was Mrs. Rose
Ashburn, 221 Herring Court, who was
found wandering the streets in the
vicinity of Lexington and Pine
Streets. The woman told officers
that she was without home. having
been ejected when she was unable to
pay rent after the death of her hus-
band three weeks ago. She was sent
to the City Hospital for three months.
The others, all men, were sent to
penal institutions after it was dis-
closed that they had migrated here
in search of work and were unable
to find any work, and were without
means of support.
