WINNIE WINKLE GIVES PROGRAM
They say that the first impression is
lasting. Well, we are quite sure that the
Winnie Winkle made a very favorable Im-
pression when they rendered their first
program at Sharon Baptist Young People's
Union Sunday. The participants consisted
largely of Douglass High School students
and also members of Enon Baptist Church.
Ruth Watts. Dorothy Douglass and Doro-
thy Green were participants from the
club. Dorothy Douglass and Mary Hall
presided.
