WHOLE BLOCK AT WORK-A scene in the 1600 block of Calhoun Street,
last week as youngsters opened the Clean Block Campaign in Baltimore by
making dirt fly. The junior department of the AFRO-AMERICAN expects
to organize clubs in every block in the city during the campaign.

ALL MAIL CARRIERS ARE NOT MALE-Miss Helen B. Gib-
son, whose four-year contract expired July 1st. She served
R.F.D. route between Marietta and Cutler, Ohio, and holds the
distinction of being the only woman in rural mail service in
the United States.
GETTING HIS DYNAMITE CHARGE READY AGAIN-The imposing figure
of Joe Louis, the Detroit Bomber, who started training in Chicago, last week,
for his bout with King Levinsky, white, at Comiskey Park, next month. Joe's
handlers expect him to explode again, making more ring history and taking a
step nearer to the heavyweight title.
