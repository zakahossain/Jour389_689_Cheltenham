Search for Three
Who Escaped
from Cheltenham
Three youths who escaped from
the Cheltenham reformatory are
among the eight persons hunted
by police and detectives of the
missing persons bureau, this week.
The boys escaped from the school
during the night and were not
missed until several hours later.
Officials of the school state
that the boys have been missing
since Tuesday.
Leonard Collins, 18, is described
as being 5 feet, 7 inches tall, 150
pounds, light brown skin. Arthur
J. Shoeman, 18, is said to be 5 feet,
6 inches tall, 125 pounds and light
brown skin. Clarence L. Howard
is 14, 5 feet, 2 inches tall, 100
pounds and dark skin.
Other persons reported missing
are:

3 inches. 170 pounds, dark skin, small
